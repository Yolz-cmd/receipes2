Margherita

Classic Italian pizza known for its simplicity and fresh flavors

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Olive OIl
  • Fresh tomatoes or tomatoe sauce
  • Olive OIl
  • Mozzarella cheese

How to make Margherita Pizza Step by Step :

  1. Preheat Oven to highest temparature
  2. Roll out pizza dough and place on a baking surface
  3. Spread tomatoe sauce, add Mozzarella slices , and scatter basil leaves
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt
  5. Bake until crust!
