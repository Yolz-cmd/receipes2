Hawaiian Pizza

Hawaiian Pizza: Sweet and savory classic

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ham or Bacon
  • Pineapple Chucks

How to make Hawaiian Step by Step :

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Spread tomatoe sauce
  3. Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese.
  4. Add ham or bacon and pineaple chunks
  5. Bake until golden and bubbky. Enjoy!
