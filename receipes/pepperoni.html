Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni Pizza: a beloved classic featuring a flavorful combination of zesty tomatoe sauce, gooey melted cheese, and spicy slices of Pepperoni

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Pepperoni SLices
  • Grated Parmesan cheese , dried oregano

How to make Hawaiian Step by Step :

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Spread tomatoe sauce
  3. Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese.
  4. Preheat oven to highest temparature
  5. Bake until the crust is golden brown Enjoy!
